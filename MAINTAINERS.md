- **[@Hei33enberg](https://github.com/Hei33enberg)** — holds the vision, the RAK mark, and the final word.

## Bureau Chiefs (area maintainers)

Area maintainers hold merge rights and a technical veto in their area. None are appointed yet — this is a pre-1.0 project. Areas that will want a Bureau Chief as the project grows:

| Area | Scope | Bureau Chief |
|---|---|---|
| Spec & profiles | `SPEC.md`, `docs/profiles/`, RFCs | _open_ |
| Skills & MCP surface | `skills/`, tool docs, `server.json` | _open_ |
| Client & registries | `bin/`, `@rak-ad/mcp`, marketplace/registry manifests | _open_ |
| Community & orchestra | `.github/`, workflows, `REALM.md` ledger | _open_ |

## Becoming a maintainer

Bureau Chief is earned, not requested: roughly **5+ substantive merged PRs and ~3 months of sustained ownership** in an area (L3 in [`REALM.md`](./REALM.md)), then Crown ratification. Heralds (L4) and Node Sovereigns (L5) are nominated by the Crown based on sustained propagation or a live production node.
