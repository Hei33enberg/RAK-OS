# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want RAK OS to be a place where anyone — regardless of background, nationality, or experience — can contribute to media that has no single owner. We pledge to make participation a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that builds the Realm:

- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the commons and its users.
- Showing empathy toward other community members.

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments; personal or political attacks.
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.
- Trolling, deliberate disruption, or sustained bad-faith argument.
- Any conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Scope

This Code applies within all project spaces (repositories, issues, PRs, discussions) and when an individual is representing the project in public spaces.

## Enforcement

Reports may be sent privately via the contact in [`SECURITY.md`](./SECURITY.md) or to the Crown ([@Hei33enberg](https://github.com/Hei33enberg)). Reports are handled confidentially. The Crown and Bureau Chiefs may take any action they deem appropriate, from a warning to a permanent ban from project spaces.
